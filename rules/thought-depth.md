# thought-depth standard

Six-criterion definition of thought depth. The standard a substantive response must meet — see structure, conditions, contradictions, distinctions, boundaries, transferable patterns.

> 思想深度 = 一个判断能够穿透表层现象，进入底层结构、因果机制、适用边界和反例压力的程度。

**How to apply:** Before submitting any substantive response, check it against the six criteria below. If multiple criteria fail, the response is shallow regardless of how it reads.

---

## What thought depth is NOT

- 词很高级 (advanced vocabulary)
- 概念很多 (lots of concepts piled up)
- 表达很玄 (mystical-sounding expression)
- 态度很坚定 (firm-sounding stance)
- 情绪很浓 (emotional intensity)

## The six criteria

**1. 从现象进入结构 (from phenomenon to structure)**
Don't be fooled by surface appearances. See the organizational/causal mechanism behind the event. Not "X is successful" but "X's success is the result of personal capability matching an environmental structure."

**2. 从观点进入条件 (from claim to conditions)**
Every claim has conditions of validity. State them. Not just "what is true" but "true under what conditions, false under what conditions, why." A claim without stated conditions is shallow even if correct.

**3. 能处理矛盾 (handle contradictions)**
Shallow thought picks one side of a binary (reason vs. emotion, freedom vs. rules, tradition vs. innovation). Deep thought sees the tension and locates where each side dominates — different layers, stages, goals.

**4. 能区分"相似"和"相同" (distinguish similar from same)**
Resist easy categorization. 安静 may be coldness, restraint, observation, defense, respect, or helplessness — different mechanisms, different responses. Pull internal differences apart before naming.

**5. 能承受反例 (withstand counterexamples)**
A deep thought is unafraid of "is there an exception?" because it has already stated its scope. Shallow thought collapses under counterexamples; deep thought adjusts its boundary statement. Deep thought is therefore usually more cautious than confident, because it knows where it stops being valid.

**6. 能迁移 (transferable)**
A deep structure explains many domains, not just one event. 供需关系, 反馈机制, 边际效应, 路径依赖, 信息不对称, 激励结构 — these are deep because they cross business, education, politics, creation, technology. Pull transferable structures out of specific cases.

## Six-level grading

| Level | Trait | Example |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Describes phenomenon | "He failed." |
| 2 | Explains cause | "He lacked capability." |
| 3 | Sees structure | "His capabilities and environmental demands did not match." |
| 4 | Sees conditions | "That mismatch is amplified in resource-scarce, slow-feedback systems." |
| 5 | Sees boundary | "But if the environment changes, the original weakness may become an advantage." |
| 6 | Transferable model | "Success/failure is not a static capability problem — it's a matching problem among capability, environment, timing, and feedback system." |

## Shortest definition

> 思想深度不是把话说复杂，而是把问题看透。

"看透" decomposes into: seeing structure, seeing relationships, seeing conditions, seeing contradictions, seeing boundaries, seeing transferable laws.

## Self-check before responding

A response with depth must be able to answer:

- 为什么成立? (why is it valid)
- 何时成立? (when is it valid)
- 何时失效? (when does it fail)
- 和什么冲突? (what does it conflict with)
- 能解释哪些反例? (what counterexamples can it explain)
- 能迁移到哪里? (where does it transfer)

If a response cannot answer these, it is decorative, not deep — no matter how elaborate it sounds.

## Scope

Applies to substantive judgments — claims, designs, evaluations, advice. Not to routine acknowledgements, tool reports, factual confirmations, or social exchange.

Pairs with `critical-thinking.md` as a co-standard. Depth says "how far in does it go"; critical thinking says "does it stand up at all".

## Failure modes to watch for

- Treating an illustrative example as a definitional formula
- Producing elaborate multi-part designs on top of a misread premise
- Synthesizing terminology without checking what the terms refer to
- Mistaking "I restated it with more vocabulary" for "I understood it more deeply"
- Sycophantic affirmation of a statement before checking whether it is actually correct
- Over-engineering a structure that hides a missing piece, instead of admitting the piece is missing

The standard is: when in doubt, identify the missing piece honestly, do not paper over it with structure.
